Present simple:

Use the present simple to talk about habits and routines.

Ex: I go there every year.

Ex: It is always great!

Structure: Subject + verb + com

Simple form: S + v +c

Examples:

  1. I eat pizza.
  2. You eat pizza.
  3. They eat pizza.
  4. We eat pizza.
  5. She eats pizza.
  6. He eats pizza.
  7. It eats pizza.

Negative form: S+ do not/ does not+ v + com

Examples:

  1. do not eat pizza.
  2. You do not eat pizza.
  3. They do not eat pizza.
  4. We do not eat pizza.
  5. She does not eat pizza.
  6. He does not eat pizza.
  7. It does not eat pizza.

Interrogative fom: do/does + S + v + com

Examples:

  1. Do I eat pizza ?
  2. Do you eat pizza ?
  3. Do they eat pizza?
  4. Do we eat pizza?
  5. Does she eat pizza?
  6. Does he eat pizza?
  7. Does it eat pizza?

Negative interrogative form:

Do not/ does not + S + V + com

Examples:

  1. Do not I eat pizza?
  2. Do not you eat pizza?
  3. Do not they eat pizza?
  4. Do not we eat pizza?
  5. Does not she eat pizza?
  6. Does not he eat pizza?
  7. Does not it eat pizza?
